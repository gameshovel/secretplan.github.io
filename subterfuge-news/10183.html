Subterfuge Build Notes

Version iOS 10183 / Android 10336

Changes:
  • Buff Infiltrator: The Infiltrator is significantly less useful than its peers and has few interesting interactions with other specialists. This change addresses both of these issues
    Before: "Drains 20 from the shield charge of any outpost it attacks."
    Now: "Drains all shield charge of any outpost it attacks."
  • Nerf Admiral: Admiral stacking breaks longer games and provides a huge tactical advantage with just one. The local bonus provides a further unnecessary edge when the Helmsman and Smuggler already exist.
    Before: "Global: Increases speed of all your subs that aren't carrying specialists by 50%. Local: Admiral travels 2x faster than ordinary subs."
    After: "Global: If you have 1 or more Admirals, increases speed of all your subs that aren't carrying specialists by 50%. Local: Admiral travels 50% faster than ordinary subs."
Balance changes will be live sometime on January 6th, 2025 for new games.
